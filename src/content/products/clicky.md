## What it does

- A Mac-native assistant that sits beside your cursor, watches your screen in real time, and answers questions you ask out loud
- Walks you through whatever tool you're in — design, video editing, music production, research
- Saying "clicky agent" spawns a background agent that builds, researches, or automates tasks on its own

## Details worth noting

- Made by Farza Majeed (of buildspace); Y Combinator Spring 2026, reportedly ~$10M raised
- The app is open-source on GitHub; runs as a menu-bar process, macOS 14.2+
- Free tier keeps full screen awareness and voice; Pro ($20/mo) adds more agent usage

## Who it's for

People curious what talking to AI looks like after the chat box.
