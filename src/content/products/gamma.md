## What it does

- Generates presentations, documents, and simple webpages from a prompt, in a card-based format
- Cards restructure and restyle by AI instruction — editing is about content, not pixel layout
- Exports to PPT and PDF, or shares as a responsive web link

## Details worth noting

- The card format adapts to any screen instead of clinging to fixed 16:9 slides
- Paid tiers add brand kits (colors, fonts, logo); free exports carry a "Made with Gamma" badge
- Credit-based AI usage — the free 400 credits cover roughly 10–15 full generations

## Who it's for

People who present often but design slides reluctantly.
