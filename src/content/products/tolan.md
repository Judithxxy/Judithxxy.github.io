## What it does

- A voice-first alien companion you talk to about your day; conversations build its persistent memory of you
- A gamified world around the character: dress up your Tolan, build out its space, invite friends
- A film-like onboarding that sets up the Tolan universe and its worldview

## Details worth noting

- Deliberately non-human characters — the company's stated stance against "artificial human mimicry"; no romance by design
- The Tolan universe, worldview, and character personalities are written by a dedicated literary lead
- Heavy engineering on the voice loop — low latency and improving memory keep the conversation smooth

## Who it's for

People who want an AI companion built as entertainment with a worldview — not a romance simulator, not another chat tool.
