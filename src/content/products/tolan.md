## What it does

- A voice-first alien companion you talk to about your day, plans, and worries
- The character develops its personality and memories from your conversations over time
- Check-ins and reflections designed around your life outside the app

## Details worth noting

- Explicitly designed against engagement farming — positioned for grounding and support, not maximizing time in app
- No romance by design — a deliberate line most companion apps won't draw; deliberately non-human characters to avoid "artificial human mimicry"
- The personality was shaped with a hired sci-fi novelist — character craft treated as seriously as the tech

## Who it's for

People curious about AI companionship but put off by the manipulative patterns of the category.
