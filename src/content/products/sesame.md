## What it does

- Real-time voice conversation with four personal agents (Maya, Miles, Simone, Charlie), each with persistent memory
- Handles interruptions and overlapping speech instead of waiting for turn-taking
- Beyond talk: search cards with images, notes for takeaways, texting mode, and longer-form "deep dives"

## Details worth noting

- The speech model generates hesitations, breath, and tone shifts in context rather than reading polished text
- Incognito mode: the agent uses context during the conversation but saves nothing to memory afterward
- Open-sourced its CSM-1B speech model (Apache 2.0); founded by an Oculus co-founder, with lightweight AI eyewear planned for 2027

## Who it's for

Anyone who wants to feel where voice interfaces are headed a year before the mainstream assistants get there.
