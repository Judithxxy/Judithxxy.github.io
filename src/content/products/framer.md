## What it does

- A no-code website builder with a free-form design canvas and instant publishing
- Wireframer generates page structure, sections, and rough copy from a prompt
- Workshop generates interactive, production-quality components that inherit the project's design system

## Details worth noting

- Hosting, CMS, SEO controls, and localization are built in — design to live site without leaving the tool
- The free plan includes AI features and a working site (with Framer badge and traffic limits)
- Grew out of a design-tool lineage, and the interaction model stays truer to Figma than to page builders

## Who it's for

Designers and founders who want a real site with design-tool control — and AI to skip the blank canvas.
