## What it does

- Hold a key, speak, release — polished text appears wherever your cursor is, in any app
- Removes filler words and self-corrections automatically instead of transcribing them
- Adapts tone and formatting to the app context, and learns your vocabulary and names over time

## Details worth noting

- Works system-wide rather than living in its own app — it meets you inside your existing tools
- Auto-detects 100+ languages; Pro adds a command mode for editing existing text by voice
- Personal dictionary picks up niche terms (product names, colleagues) over time; voice snippets expand into saved formatted text

## Who it's for

People who write a lot of messages and docs and think faster than they type.
