## What it does

- Upload documents, web pages, and video links, then ask questions, summarize, and outline against them
- Every answer is annotated with sources; clicking a citation jumps to the original passage
- Audio Overviews turns your sources into a two-host podcast episode

## Details worth noting

- Asked something outside your sources, it says the sources don't cover it instead of inventing an answer
- Handles non-English sources, including podcast generation in other languages
- The free tier covers most personal research use

## Who it's for

Students, researchers, and product people who need to digest piles of documents and can't afford made-up answers.
